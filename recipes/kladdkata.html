Kladdkaka

Description

Kladdkaka or the Swedish sticky chocolate cake!
Is a delicious dessert that will be done in under 30 minutes.

ingredients

  • 150 g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 dl sugar
  • 3.5 dl flour
  • 1 dl cocoa
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar
  • salt

Steps

  1. Melt the butter

  2. Whisk the sugar and eggs lightly and stir in the butter.

  3. Mix flour, cocoa, vanilla sugar and salt and stir into the batter.

  4. Pour the batter in a pan and bake it at 200 degree celcius for about 12 minutes.
