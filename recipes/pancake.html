Pancakes

Description

Thin Swedish pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 dl milk
  • 1.5 dl flour
  • salt
  • 2 tablespoon melted butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs and milk and stir down the flour, salt and melted butter.

  2. Take 1-2 dl of the pancake batter and fry the pancake golden on both sides.
